## What this is

A one-page canvas with four columns (L1–L4) and three rows: *what we'll measure*, *how we'll capture it*, *who owns the signal*. It forces the evaluation conversation to happen at kickoff, not after launch.

## How to use it

1. Fill it out with your sponsor before Design begins.
2. Every measure gets a data source **and** an owner.
3. If the L3 or L4 columns come out blank, that's a signal — usually the business metric isn't actually tied to the behavior we're training.

## Why I like it

It surfaces the uncomfortable conversation early: *can we actually attribute behavior change to this training, or are we just hoping?* Every program I've run improves the moment that question is on the table.
