## What this is

A small, ordered set of prompts I use in Cursor to draft a branching scenario from SME interview notes. Nothing magical about any single prompt — the value is in what gets pinned to context between each one, and in what I *don't* ask the model to decide.

## Who it's for

Learning experience designers who already know what good scenario design looks like, and want to stop typing the first eighty percent of it by hand.

## How it'll work

1. **Discovery prompt** — paste SME interview notes, get back a clean problem statement.
2. **Decision-point extraction prompt** — surface 3-5 candidate moments where a learner chooses.
3. **Distractor-and-feedback prompt** — produce a scenario draft with Rise-ready feedback blocks.
4. **Voice pass** — that part's still you.

The prompts will live on this page and as a Notion-ready template.
