## What this is

Google Vids landed on my desk as an alternative to Camtasia for quick training explainers and microlearning intros. After a few weeks of using it in real projects, here's where it's earned a spot in the stack — and where I still reach for something else.

## Where it wins

- First-draft speed for talking-head + slide walkthroughs
- Native Workspace access for SMEs to review
- AI-assisted scripting that actually gets close to usable on pass one

## Where it doesn't

- Any sequence that needs precise frame-level timing
- Branching or interactive overlays
- Accessibility captions at the quality bar I want

## What I'm using it for today

Quick-turn intro and outro bumpers, SME interview edits, pilot-program comms videos. Not for anything I'd mount inside a scored course.
